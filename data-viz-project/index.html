Litter Collected in Prince George's County

Diving Into Litter by Location

By Lillian Glaros

This visualization takes a look at the number of bags of litter collected by Prince George's County residents, community organizations and businesses during individual and group clean-up events. It explores how many bags were collected in county council districts and major watersheds from Fiscal Year 2023-2025. This data can show what areas have the most litter, although it does not take into account the number of clean-up efforts per area.
